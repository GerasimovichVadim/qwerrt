About the travel agency

Travel agents do not own the means of service and act as intermediaries between the tourist service company and the buyer of a tourist voucher, promoting and selling a tourist product. The legislation fixing the activities of travel agencies and tour operators in the Russian Federation can be found on the website of the Federal Agency for Tourism. In Russia, the tourism business began to develop actively in the 1990s. In 1993, the Russian Association of Travel Agencies (RATA) was established, which in 2002 was transformed into the Russian Union of Travel Industry (PCT)[4]. One of the problems in the sphere of legal regulation of relations between insurance companies and tour operators is the absence of a legislative ban on changing the status of companies that sell tourist products to end users (tourists).
